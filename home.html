Who we are

The main mission of ConnexUs is to help people connect, all over the world, while cherishing language pride. ConnexUs strives to eliminate language barriers by providing a platform where users can connect with others who speak a language that they are trying to learn.

How to

In order to use ConnexUs, proceed to the chat page. There, you will enter your preferences including what language you are trying to learn. From there, you wil be connected with someone. After that, start chatting!
